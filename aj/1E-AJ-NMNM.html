Nové město na Moravě

Nové město na Moravě is small town in the centre of Žďárské vrchy. The town has about 9800 inhabitants, but with nearest villages it has about 11 000 inhabitants. The town was found by Boček z Obřan as a village for the monastery of Zelená hora in 14th century. The town got privileges from Vratisalv from Pernštejn such as making beer, throaty law, market law and others. The town organizes sports events events for example race of bikes, biathlon race, zlatá lyže (the golden ski race). The town has two churches and one church is painted as some other houses in Nové Město by Karel Němec. One year left in Nové Město, there was built a swimming pool with wellness. We have two train stations, public transport, two schools on three pieces, library, a few restaurants, three chemists, one helicopter pad and at least one drug dealer.
